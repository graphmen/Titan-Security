💡 Pro Tips for Your Build
Offline-first mobile app — Guards often work in basements or areas with poor signal. Cache NFC scans + GPS locally, sync when online
Anti-fraud measures — Prevent GPS spoofing (mock location detection), prevent NFC tag cloning, require biometric (fingerprint/face) on clock-in
Battery optimization — Continuous GPS drains battery fast. Use balanced accuracy modes and adaptive intervals
Geofence shapes — Use polygons (not just circles) for complex premise shapes
Timestamp integrity — Use server-side timestamps, not device time (guards can change phone clocks)
Tamper detection — If the app is closed or phone is turned off mid-shift, flag it immediately
